Principles
Design Principles
The rules that govern every design decision in this system.
Borders, not shadows
Elevation is conveyed through border contrast and background layering, not drop shadows. This creates a crisp, engineering-grade interface.
Density with clarity
Every pixel earns its place. Compact spacing, small font sizes, but never at the cost of readability. Information-dense, not cluttered.
Professional restraint
No gradients, no glow effects, no playful animations. The design reflects the seriousness of legal work -- composed, confident, and precise.
