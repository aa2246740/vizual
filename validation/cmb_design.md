Design System: China Merchants Bank
1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
China Merchants Bank's brand identity is built on the brand style of "sincere and optimistic, smart and appropriate, brilliant and modern, sharp and genuine." The design takes CMB Red (Pantone 193) and CMB Black as the core visual elements, organizing basic visual elements such as graphics, text, and colors in an orderly manner to output a complete "visual language" and building CMB's visual identity system.
The typography uses Founder Lanting Hei (方正兰亭黑) — a professional Chinese typeface family that is clean, modern, and highly legible. The font operates in multiple weight ranges: Regular for body text, Medium for secondary headings, and Bold for primary headings. Combined with Arial for English text, the typeface creates a professional, trustworthy banking identity.
What distinguishes CMB is its disciplined color system and standardized logo usage. The primary brand color is the distinctive CMB Red (Pantone 193 / #c8152d), representing vitality and enthusiasm, symbolizing the bank's operational energy and service enthusiasm. Combined with the official sunflower pattern (向日葵) — symbolizing "customers are the sun, CMB is the sunflower" — the brand creates a warm, customer-centric impression.
Key Characteristics:
- Pure white canvas with CMB Red (Pantone 193 / #c8152d) as the primary brand color
- Founder Lanting Hei (方正兰亭黑) — official Chinese typeface family
- Arial — designated English typeface for consistency
- Sunflower pattern (向日葵) — brand flower symbolizing customer-centric service
- Strict color system: Pantone values, CMYK, and RGB all precisely defined
- Grid-based logo composition with protected clear space

4. Brand Logo System
4.1 Bank Emblem Design
The bank emblem uses China Merchants Bank's English name initials C, M, B as basic design elements, representing CMB's international and modern image.
Design Meaning:
- The visual center "M" is solid and powerful, symbolizing CMB's development momentum in expanding domestic and international markets
- The horizontal line below "M" forms "B" while creating a graphic meaning of sailing out to sea and smooth capital flow together with speed-like parallel rays
- Seven parallel rays represent CMB's seven original shareholders, bearing distinct era characteristics
Standard Color: Red representing vitality and enthusiasm, symbolizing the bank's operational energy and service enthusiasm, full of upward momentum and proactive market response attitude.
The entire emblem reflects the spirit of continuous progress and transformation force, conveying the brand philosophy of "changing for you" (因您而变), perfectly integrating with the brand's characteristics and cultural qualities.
4.2 Brand Logo Composition
The brand logo consists of three elements:
1. Bank Emblem — Red circular "M" graphic symbol
2. Chinese Name — "招商银行" (China Merchants Bank) written by renowned calligrapher Qin Qinsheng
3. English Name — "CHINA MERCHANTS BANK" based on Times extrabold with design refinements
Critical Principle: The brand logo must use electronic files provided in the appendix. The logo must not be modified in any way, including any proportional adjustments, deformations, or redrawing.
